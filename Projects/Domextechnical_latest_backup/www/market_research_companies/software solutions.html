    Logistics Industry Software

  • Domex has developed a series of comprehensive software solutions to cater to a variety of needs of the logistics industry and more specifically : Public Bonded Warehouses ; Container Freight Stations & Inland Container Depots. Software can be customized to individual user requirements. Many logistics companies in TN / Southern India are profitably utilizing Domex design software.

    Public Bonded Warehouses Software

    This software comes in 3 distinct modules for maintenance, bonding and de-bonding and has been designed to provide a host of MIS reports to the warehouse owners / operators. Highlights of this software are listed below :

    • Maintenance Module : enables capturing of details related to customs house agents (CHA), rates, warehouses and insurance
    • Bonding Module : covers application for bonds and the processed involving bonding
    • De-bonding Module : covers de-bonding, gate pass, receipt printing, receipts and payables.
    • Reporting : Multiple reports are generated by the software to meet a variety of MIS requirements of the warehouse owners and management. Typical reports generated by the software include :
    Receipt Register Allotment Register Despatch Register Rent & Final Insurance
    Stock Report (CHA-wise) Stock Report (Pack-wise) Agent-wise Pending Stock Bond-wise Pending Stock
    Agent-wise Transactions List Bond-wise Transactions List Discounts (CHA-wise) Handling Charges
    Rent Accruals (period-wise) Bond Details Report Gate Pass Monthly Report

    CFS & ICD Monitoring Software

    This software has been specifically designed for application in container freight stations and inland container depots and will enable the monitoring of the entire container movement. The software is eminently suitable for providing logistic support and the planning of movement of containers. Further, the program generates a host of details that includes age-wise analysis and position of containers. The software has 4 basic modules as listed below :

    • Entry Module : enables capturing of details related to customs house agents (CHA),shipping lines, rates and equipment.
    • Import Module : captureof details such as unloading of containers, inspection by Customs and de-stuffing.
    • Export Module : covers capture of details such as unloading cargo, stuffing and issue of exit gate pass.
    • Empty Containers Module : covers capture of details related to empty containers taken up for storing.
    • Reporting : Multiple reports are generated by the software to meet a variety of MIS requirements of the CFS / ICD owners and management. Typical reports generated by the software include :
    Import Container Arrival Import Container Despatch (Factory De-stuffing) CHA-wise / Shipping line-wise
    PNR-wise Container Arrival Customs Inspection Export Cargo Arrival
    Export Container Despatch Export CHA-wise Equipment Utilization

    Inventory Control Software

    This software has been designed to monitor stock movements and is useful for planning stocks and inventory and the corresponding sales. The software provides details of stock in bonding or godowns by variety, age and volume / quantity. The software can be customized for any complex product range and its inventory management. The software has 4 basic modules as listed below :

    • Entry Module : capturing of details related to customers, materials, rates and such other items.
    • Outgoing Purchase Orders : captureof details such as unloading of containers, inspection by Customs and de-stuffing.
    • Bonding: covers capture of details related to bonding of stock, de-bonding of stocks and excise duty payments.
    • Sales Module : helps capture details on income purchase orders, spot printing of delivery challans, invoice generation and sales return.
    • Reporting : Multiple reports are generated by the software to meet a variety of MIS requirements of the owners and management of manufacturing units, distributors, agents and others associated with shipment of goods.
