About Us

Overview History Management

  • Domex provides authentic information for your strategy planning, business growth and decision making. We have been a pioneer in researching & compiling usable information on 25 key sectors of Indian economy and industry. We have also begun to provide customized software for the logistic industry and marketing / sales personnel involved in capital equipment and industrial components & spares.

    Domex directors by virtue of their diverse experience across several business segments is in a position to bring to the table huge advantages that can benefit its customers and information database subscribers. We highlight below some expertise areas that we have assimilated over the past 3 decades.

    • Over 15 years of sustained experience in handling large volume extended term outsourcing contracts for globally renowned database and publishing companies – some of them billion dollar companies. Some of the key contracts have lasted tenures ranging between 5 and 15 years. Until mid 2010, Domex founders / directors were handling an intellectual and knowledge workforce of nearly 500 engineers, business analysts and science post graduates.
    • Over 20 years of international marketing experience spread over East Asia, EU / UK and US. Domex directors could successfully identify prospective customers and negotiate good deals related to a host of KPO, BPO and consultancy services. Domex could obtain contracts from international companies in highly competitive environment by formulating appropriate marketing strategies and customer relationships.
    • Delivered processed / fully researched and analyzed information conforming to global quality standards and that too within rigid and shortened time frames. Worked to meet deliveries to customers and recipients in various time zones – Japan, US, UK and EU.
    • Can recruit / mobilize and train a large intellectual workforce within quick time to handle challenging assignments. Built significant HR expertise to recruit graduates / post graduates and put them through fast track training and their induction into the production stream.
    • Built significant expertise in trade fair participation that included booth set up, stand design, exhibit design, pre-exhibition publicity to bring foot falls, engagement of visitors, conversion of enquiries into tangible business, post-exhibition follow-ups and such other matters.
    • Conceived and provided leadership to develop a variety of software tools to extract, document and analyze information drawn from diverse sources including field surveys and secondary information. Software tools development is an ongoing activity at Domex to meet the information explosion, Internet and Database searching challenges.
    • Ability to deliver high quality work within truncated time frames at highly attractive prices.

    Domex also provides handholding services for foreign businessmen entering the Indian market.

  • Domex was initially established in 1988 as a firm to research, compile, collate and disseminate information, business leads and contacts database on various sectors of the Indian economy and industry. Domex can rightly claim its pioneering status in providing new projects information on 40 key industry sectors. As this new projects monitoring service gained popularity, it came to be known as an ‘industrial marketing tool’ by sales and marketing professionals in the Indian industry. Over 3,000 Indian companies including MNCs currently subscribe to Domex’s new projects information service.

    Over the years, Domex, by virtue of its experience in researching markets, technological developments and new projects monitoring diversified into knowledge processing / KPO services. Between 1994 and 2010 (16 years), Domex Technical Information (DTI) and its offshoot, Domex e-Data (450 employee base) provided high quality research, editorial, abstracting, indexing and data conversion services in the science, technical & medicine (STM) and business research segment besides offering a host of BPO and prepress services to globally renowned database, information and publishing companies in Europe and North America. Domex e-Data and its core business of STM abstracting & indexing was acquired by Molecular Connections in July 2010.

    DTI and its subsidiary DBI was promoted in 1995 by a group of information, marketing and IT professionals. The company is headed by Mr P V Satyanarayana, a well-known KPO / BPO specialist with nearly four decades experience in KPO / BPO activities, international marketing, market research and economic and business information. DTI has its production centers at Chennai, Coimbatore and Mumbai besides representative and correspondents’ offices at Pune, Kolkata and Delhi.
